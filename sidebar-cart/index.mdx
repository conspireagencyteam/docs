Bonde's sidebar cart (also called a cart drawer) provides a feature-rich cart experience that integrates with all of Bonde's features.

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Upsell display** | Show cart upsells directly in the sidebar |
| **Bundle grouping** | Bundle items appear grouped together with expand/collapse |
| **Subscription display** | Shows delivery frequency for subscription items |
| **Customizable styling** | Match your theme's look and feel |
| **Smooth animations** | Modern slide-in/out behavior |

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app embed">
    Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize**, click **App Embeds**, and enable the **Bonde** app embed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the sidebar cart">
    In the Bonde app, go to **Settings > Upsells > Sidebar Cart** and toggle on the sidebar cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Customize the sidebar cart appearance and behavior in the settings panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration options

In **Settings > Upsells > Sidebar Cart**, you can configure:

- **Enable/disable**: Turn the sidebar cart on or off
- **Styling**: Colors, fonts, and spacing to match your theme
- **Upsell display**: Whether to show upsells in the cart and how many
- **Bundle display**: How bundle items are grouped and displayed

## Cart upsells

When you create cart upsells in Bonde, they automatically appear in the sidebar cart. The upsells are triggered based on cart contents and can include discounts.

See [Cart upsells](/upsells/cart) for details on creating upsell offers.

## Bundle display

When customers add bundles to their cart, the sidebar cart:

- Groups all bundle items together under a header
- Shows the bundle name and total price
- Allows expand/collapse to see individual items
- For subscription bundles, shows the delivery frequency

## Subscription display

For subscription items, the sidebar cart shows:

- The subscription frequency (e.g., "Delivers every 30 days")
- Any subscription discount applied
- Visual grouping for subscription bundles

## Troubleshooting

**Sidebar cart not appearing?**
- Make sure the Bonde app embed is enabled in your theme
- Check that the sidebar cart is enabled in **Settings > Upsells > Sidebar Cart**
- Some themes have their own cart drawers that may conflict—you may need to disable the theme's cart drawer

**Cart upsells not showing?**
- Verify you have active cart upsells configured
- Check that the trigger conditions match the current cart contents

**Bundle items not grouped?**
- Ensure bundles are added through the Bonde bundle widget
- Check that the sidebar cart is properly initialized
