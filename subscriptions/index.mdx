Bonde's subscription system lets you offer recurring orders with flexible intervals, subscriber discounts, and customer self-service management.

## Features

- **Flexible intervals**: Offer subscriptions that renew every 15 days, 30 days, 60 days, or any interval
- **Subscriber discounts**: Give subscribers a percentage or fixed amount discount
- **Customer self-service**: Customers can skip deliveries, pause, swap products, and manage their subscriptions
- **Bundle subscriptions**: Offer subscriptions for build-a-box bundles
- **Order consolidation**: Combine multiple subscriptions into single shipments
- **Automated emails**: Keep subscribers informed with configurable email notifications

## How it works

1. You configure subscription plans for your products
2. Customers see subscription options on product pages
3. When they subscribe, a subscription contract is created
4. Bonde automatically creates orders at each billing interval
5. Customers manage their subscriptions through the customer portal
