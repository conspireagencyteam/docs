description: "Set up recurring orders with Bonde's subscription features."
---

Bonde's subscription system lets you offer recurring orders with flexible intervals and discounts.

## What's included

- Configurable billing intervals (every 15 days, 30 days, etc.)
- Subscription discounts (percentage or fixed amount)
- Customer self-service portal for managing subscriptions
- Automated email notifications
- Support for bundle subscriptions

## Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app embed">
    Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize**, click **App Embeds**, and enable the **Bonde** app embed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure subscription plans">
    In the Bonde app, go to **Subscriptions > Products** and configure selling plans for your products. See [Subscription configuration](/subscriptions/configuration) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the product page widget">
    Add the **Bonde: Subscription PDP** app block to your product template. See [Product widget](/subscriptions/product-widget) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the customer portal">
    Add the **Bonde: Customer Subscriptions** extension to customer accounts. See [Customer portal](/subscriptions/customer-portal) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
