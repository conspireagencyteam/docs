---

Bonde's order tracking pages give your customers a branded shipment tracking experience on your own domain, with product recommendations and optional discount offers.

## Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first tracking page">
    In the Bonde app, go to **Tracking** and click **Create Page**.

    Add blocks to your page — at minimum, include a **Tracking status** block and an **Order items** block.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your theme">
    Set your logo, brand colors, and typography in the theme editor. Your Shopify brand logo is automatically detected if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add product recommendations (optional)">
    Add a **Shop again** block and create **Order Tracking** upsells in the Upsells tab. Assign them to the block to show product recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable discount codes (optional)">
    In the Shop again block settings, toggle on **Offer a discount code** and configure the discount type, value, and optional expiry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the page">
    Toggle the page to **Active**. Tracking URLs are automatically generated for new fulfillments.
  </Step>
</Steps>
