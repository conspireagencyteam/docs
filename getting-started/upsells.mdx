Bonde's upsell system lets you display targeted product offers at multiple touchpoints to increase average order value.

## Upsell types

| Type | Where it shows | Best for |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **Product page** | On individual product pages | Complementary products and accessories |
| **Cart** | In the sidebar cart or cart page | Add-ons before checkout |
| **Checkout** | During checkout (Shopify Plus only) | Last-minute additions |
| **Post-purchase** | After checkout, before thank you page | One-click upsells |
| **Order tracking** | On branded tracking pages | Repeat purchase offers with discount codes |

## Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app embed">
    Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize**, click **App Embeds**, and enable the **Bonde** app embed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add upsell widgets to your theme">
    Depending on which upsell types you want, add the appropriate app blocks to your theme:
    - **Product page**: Add **Bonde: Product Page Upsell** to your product template
    - **Cart**: Enable the [sidebar cart](/sidebar-cart/index) or add the widget to your cart page
    - **Checkout**: Add the checkout upsell block (Shopify Plus only)
    - **Post-purchase**: Enable the post-purchase extension in checkout settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create upsell offers">
    In the Bonde app, go to **Upsells** and create your upsell offers with trigger conditions and discounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
