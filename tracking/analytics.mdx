---
title: "Tracking analytics"
description: "Monitor tracking page performance and recommendation engagement."
---

Bonde tracks page views and product recommendation clicks on your tracking pages, giving you insight into customer engagement.

## Tracked events

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Page view** | Recorded each time a customer visits a tracking page |
| **Upsell click** | Recorded when a customer clicks a recommended product |

## Viewing analytics

Navigate to **Tracking > Analytics** in the Bonde app to see:

- Total page views over time
- Recommendation click-through rates
- Performance by tracking page (if you have multiple pages with targeting rules)

## Tips for improving engagement

- **Add a discount**: Tracking pages with discount offers see higher click-through rates on product recommendations
- **Use expiring discounts**: A countdown timer creates urgency and drives faster action
- **Keep recommendations relevant**: Use trigger conditions to show products related to what the customer ordered
- **Try intelligent mode**: Let Bonde's recommendation engine find the best products based on your order history
