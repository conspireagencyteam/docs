title: "Product recommendations"
description: "Display upsell offers on your tracking page to drive repeat purchases."
---

The **Shop again** block on your tracking page shows product recommendations to encourage repeat purchases. You can hand-pick products, use intelligent recommendations powered by purchase data, or combine both.

## How recommendations work

Product recommendations on the tracking page are powered by the same upsell system used across Bonde. Create **Order Tracking** upsells in the Upsells tab, then assign them to a Shop again block on your tracking page.

Each product displays with an image, title, price, and an **Add** button that takes the customer directly to their cart.

## Setting up recommendations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Order Tracking upsell">
    Go to **Upsells** and click **Create Upsell**. Select **Order Tracking** as the type.

    Add trigger conditions (optional) and select the products you want to recommend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Shop again block">
    In the tracking page builder, add a **Shop again** block to your page layout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select upsells for the block">
    In the block settings, choose which Order Tracking upsells feed this block. Leave empty to show all active Order Tracking upsells.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set max products">
    Configure how many product recommendations to show (1-8). Defaults to 4.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recommendation modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Manual** | Hand-picked products from your upsell offers. Best when you know exactly what to recommend. |
| **Intelligent** | Auto-selected based on co-purchase patterns. Products are A/B tested and optimized over time. |
| **Hybrid** | Manual offers show first, intelligent fills remaining slots. Best of both worlds. |

To use intelligent mode, mark your Order Tracking upsell as **Intelligent** when creating it. Bonde analyzes your order history to find products frequently bought together and continuously optimizes which products are shown.

## Trigger conditions

Order Tracking upsells support the same trigger conditions as other upsell types:

- **Product in order**: Show when specific products were in the order
- **Cart value minimum**: Show when the order total exceeds an amount
- **Collection**: Show when products from a specific collection were in the order
