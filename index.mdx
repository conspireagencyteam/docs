Bonde is a Shopify app that helps you increase average order value and customer lifetime value with powerful bundling, subscription, upsell, and loyalty features.

## Features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bundles" icon="box" href="/bundles/index">
    Sell product packs, curated sets, and build-a-box bundles with tiered discounts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscriptions" icon="rotate" href="/subscriptions/index">
    Offer recurring orders with flexible intervals, discounts, and customer self-service.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upsells" icon="arrow-up-right" href="/upsells/index">
    Display targeted offers on product pages, cart, checkout, and post-purchase.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sidebar cart" icon="cart-shopping" href="/sidebar-cart/index">
    A customizable cart drawer with upsells, bundle grouping, and subscription display.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Loyalty" icon="gift" href="/loyalty/index">
    Reward customers with store credit for purchases, subscriptions, and referrals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email notifications" icon="envelope" href="/emails/notifications">
    Automated emails for subscription events with custom templates and branding.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Quick start

Get up and running with Bonde in three steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app embed">
    Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize**, click **App Embeds** in the left sidebar, and enable the **Bonde** app embed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a feature to set up">
    Pick a feature to start with and follow its installation guide:
    - [Bundles installation](/getting-started/bundles)
    - [Subscriptions installation](/getting-started/subscriptions)
    - [Upsells installation](/getting-started/upsells)
    - [Sidebar cart installation](/getting-started/sidebar-cart)
    - [Loyalty installation](/getting-started/loyalty)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and launch">
    Follow the feature-specific guide to configure your settings and go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
