---
title: "Checkout upsells"
description: "Display upsell offers during checkout to increase average order value."
---

Checkout upsells display offers to customers during the checkout process, right before they complete their purchase.

<Warning>
Checkout upsells are only available for Shopify Plus stores.
</Warning>

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open checkout customization">
    Navigate to **Settings > Checkout > Customize** in your Shopify admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select checkout page">
    Use the tab in the top middle to select your checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the app block">
    Add the Bonde checkout upsell app block. We recommend placing it below the order summary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a checkout upsell

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the upsell">
    In the Bonde app, go to **Upsells** and click **Create Upsell**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select type">
    Select **Checkout** as the upsell type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure triggers">
    Configure trigger conditions to control when the upsell appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select product">
    Select the product offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add discount (optional)">
    Optionally configure a discount—you can offer different discounts at checkout than other areas of your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>
